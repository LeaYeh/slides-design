Neutral scale — the hierarchy

Text hierarchy is built from Ink → Graphite → Slate → Silver. No pure black; #1D1D1F reads softer, more Apple.
Theme ramp — teal (your color, extended for contrast)
Teal 900#0B5F60 · deep bg
Teal 800#0A6E6F · accent text
Teal 600 ★#22B5B7 · primary fill
Teal 400#6FD6D8 · mid
Teal 300#A2E8E9 · highlight
Teal 100#E8FBFC · tint surface
Your three anchors are 600 / 300 / 100. I added 800/900 (darker) because #22B5B7 on white is too low-contrast for text — accent text uses Teal 800; fills/bars/shapes use Teal 600; highlight background uses Teal 100/300.
